Pricing

When you request a commission from me, I will send over an estimated price based on how long the commission will take to complete and complexity. Before beginning, I will require a down payment of 1/3 of this estimated price. Once I am halfway done with the job, I will then require another payment of 1/3, totaling to 2/3 at this time. Once here, you may request to be sent all code completed up to this time, only after I have recieved the second payment. Once the commission is complete, the final price will be sent over, and once the full price is covered (remainder from what has already been sent), I will send over the completed project via a ROBLOX place file (.rbxl). Any bugs that you encounter I will be happy to patch for you for no extra cost.
